---

### **Interactive Features**

**The Mischievous "No" Button:**
- When the user hovers over the "No" button, it quickly jumps to a random position on the screen
- The button stays within the visible area but becomes impossible to click
- Each escape attempt adds to the playful frustration!

**The "Yes" Button:**
- Stays perfectly still and welcoming
- Has a gentle pulsing/glowing animation to draw attention
- Becomes the obvious (and only real) choice

---

### **Celebration When "Yes" is Clicked**

- Hearts and confetti burst across the entire screen
